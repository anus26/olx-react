    Lyari area of ​​Karachi has become the battleground again

    Fire and grenade attack different areas of in Lyari, people were forced to migrate.

    Lyari area of Karachi has become the battleground again. Collision between two groups happened on Al-Falah Road. Al-Falah Road, Jhatpat market and other areas Resonance rise of firing and also there were two IED attack. Due to Shootings and grenade attacks atmosphere of panic spread in area. 

    Process of migration of lyari residents have been started again. Migration are being started from Slaughter-house, Shah Beg and other areas.
